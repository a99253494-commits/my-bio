Chickhen Biryani Recipe

Click here

Ingredients

.2 cups basmati chawal
.500g chicken 
.2 piyaz (barik sliced)
.2 tamatar (chopped)
.1 cup dahi
.2 tbsp adrak lehsan paste
.3–4 hari mirch
.1/2 cup cooking oil
.1 tsp haldi powder
.1 tsp lal mirch powder
.1 tsp dhania powder
.1/2 tsp garam masala
.Namak hasb-e-zaiqa
.Sabut garam masala (elaichi, dalchini, laung)
.Hara dhania aur pudina (garnish ke liye)
.2–3 aloo (optional)
.Zafran ya food color (optional)

👩‍🍳 Recipe / Description:

Sab se pehle chawal ko achi tarah dho kar 30 minutes ke liye bhigo dein. Ek pan mein oil garam karein aur piyaz ko golden brown hone tak fry karein.
Ab ismein adrak lehsan paste daal kar halka sa bhunein, phir chicken ya beef add karein aur achi tarah se cook karein jab tak color change ho jaye.
Ab tamatar, dahi, haldi, lal mirch, dhania powder aur namak daal kar medium heat par pakayein jab tak masala oil chhorne lage.
Agar aloo use kar rahe hain to is stage par add karein. Dusri side par chawal ko namak aur sabut garam masala ke sath 70% tak boil kar lein.
Ab ek degchi mein layer banayein: pehle chawal, phir masala, phir chawal. Upar se zafran ya food color, hara dhania aur pudina daal dein.
Degchi ko dhak kar 10–15 minutes dum par rakh dein. Jab biryani ready ho jaye to halki si mix karein aur garma garam serve karein.

😋 Mazedar, khushbudaar biryani tayyar hai!
